KEY INSIGHTS FROM SALES DASHBOARD

1. REGIONAL CONCENTRATION
   Central region generates 22% of total sales ($2.82M), while West 
   region lags at only $0.73M, indicating significant growth opportunity 
   in underperforming markets.

2. SEASONAL PATTERNS
   November sales peak at $1.53M (2.8x higher than February's $0.54M), 
   demonstrating clear holiday season impact requiring strategic inventory 
   planning for Q4.

3. BALANCED CATEGORY MIX
   Technology leads at 37.5% market share, but all three categories 
   (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies) contribute 30%+, showing 
   healthy revenue diversification and reduced business risk.
